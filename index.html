Hello! My name is Sushrita (suh-shree-tah) and I'm a senior at the University of Michigan. I'm currently a part of the BLABLABLAB here at UMich and was previously a part of the Affective Computing Group at the University of Southern California.

I'll be applying as a PhD student this time of year! If you're interested, please reach out! I've been fortunate enough to be mentored by great minds like David Jurgens, Jonathan Gratch, and Hua Shen. My primary interests lie in LLM interpretability, the intersection of NLP and HCI, and understanding how we can make more trustworthy AI systems that mimic human behavior. I'm a strong believer that humans must be understood in order to artificiate their behavior and I intend to make this a large focus of my research questions!
